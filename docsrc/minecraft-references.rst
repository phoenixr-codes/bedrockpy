================
Target Selectors
================

.. list-table::
   
   * - **@s**
     - entity executing the command
   
   * - **@p**
     - nearest player
   
   * - **@a**
     - all players
   
   * - **@e**
     - all entities
   
   * - **@r**
     - random player

*... and a few more irrelevant ones ...*

You may also just use the player's name.
